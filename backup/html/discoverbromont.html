VISIT THE BROMONT REGION


WSki Bromont, located on Mont Brome, is a mountain with a spectacular view overlooking one of the most beautiful valleys in the Eastern Townships.

When visiting Bromont, you will discover its breathtaking natural beauty and a host of activities, whether you are a family, a couple or with friends.

If you decide to spend more than one day in the region, lodging is available, and if you have a Ski Bromont season pass, you will find interesting packages and deals. Your stay will be unforgettable.
