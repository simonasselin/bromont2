BIKE WASH STATION


Forgot your socks at home? Never mind: check out our shop! It has a great stock of clothes, parts and accessories: gloves, jerseys, neck guards, eyewear, helmets and much more!

Need equipment? You can rent anything you need: single or dual suspension bikes, helmets, knee, elbow and neck guards, etc. Everything receives daily maintenance to make sure you have a great experience.

Two bike wash stations are available on site : in parking lot P1 and P5, near the temporary parking areas. This service is free: enjoy!



PARKING

LOCKERS

BIKE REPAIR SHOP


There are numerous parking alternatives around the mountain, including parking areas P1 and P2, located close to the Main Chalet. Parking is free.

Starting on Victoria Day, the large parking area at the Versant du Lac (P5), accessible via Huntington Street, offers 700 spaces on weekends and holidays. It has all the necessary services (chalet, ticket counter, restrooms, bike wash, etc.), and its proximity to a quad chairlift may be a key factor in choosing your route. To familiarize yourself with the parking areas, consult the map and plan of the streets around the mountain.

You can rent a locker for the day at Ski Bromont at a cost of $5* for a small locker and $10** for a larger one. Keys can be picked up at the Customer Service Department.

*You get $2 back when you return the key the same day.

**You get $5 back when you return the key the same day.

Did you know that it's important to give your bike a tune-up at beginning each season? Ride worry-free by also getting regular brake and gear maintenance. Our repair shop, located in the Rental Department, offers various maintenance packages for beginners and experts alike. Let our team guide you!
