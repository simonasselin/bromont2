Following requests from many parents, we are happy to announce that the Ski School will once again offer a ski and snowboard camp during March Break, from March 4 to 6, 2014!

For 3 days, your kids will get to learn from experienced, passionate ski and snowboard instructors and spend all that extra energy hitting the slopes, while fine-tuning their technique.

Packages are priced at $365 and include group lessons (minimum 3 students, maximum 5) from 9 am to 12 pm and from 1 pm to 3 pm, plus a lunch box at noon. The camp is open to young people ages 6 to 17 of all ski and snowboard levels.

Lift tickets are not included. If your child does not have a valid season pass for the lessons, you can purchase a special spring break pass for the 3-day camp at a price of $75. Available at the Ski School counter.

For more information or to reserve a place at this year's
“Spring into Action for March Break” camp,
contact the Ski School at 1 866 276-6668, ext. 2700.
